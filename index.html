Welcome to my portfolio!

My love for electronics started at the age of 7. I would take apart radio-controlled cars and attempt to figure out how they worked. By 14 years old, I had built my first computer, was a part of my school's electronics club, and was a participant of the College Now program offered by my High School.

I continued my post-secondary studies by partaking in a work-study scholarship with the Republic of Korea's National Institute for International Education (TaLK division). This wonderful opportunity allowed me to work towards earning my Bachelor of Science Degree while traveling the world and teaching underprivileged, rural students English.

Currently, I work as an Embedded Systems Software Engineer at Radio Sound, Inc. and remotely take Embedded Systems Engineering Masters degree courses at the University of Colorado Boulder. In my downtime I continue to use what I've learned to make electronics for my own personal enjoyment and improvements. I’m dedicated to learning all things technology that interact with the world, and welcome any opportunity that allows me to strengthen my skills and use my acquired knowledge.
