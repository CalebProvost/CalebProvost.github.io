Welcome to my portfolio!

Equipped with a robust skill set, I am an experienced embedded systems software engineer with a proven track record of designing and implementing robust solutions for diverse applications, including consumer electronics, automotive electronics, and industrial automation. Proficient in embedded C/C++, real-time operating systems, and hardware-software integration, I excel at delivering efficient and scalable embedded software solutions. My commitment to professional growth is evident in my ability to earn a Master's degree while working full-time, enhancing my skills in collaborating cross-functionally and translating complex requirements into optimized software solutions.

Throughout my career, I have successfully troubleshooted intricate hardware-software integration issues and delivered innovative embedded solutions that leverage the latest technologies, ensuring high performance and reliability.
